 I built a [ThreeJS](https://threejs.org/) model uploader and viewer and using [socket.io](https://socket.io/) users to chat about the model, share the mouse to move the model to show what they were chatting about for a node knockout 48 hour hackathon. This was an interesting project, pursuing the interaction between the users and how the handing off use of the camera in the world could work. I also added something where you could see the other users mouse as well, this something that you see in figma today.
