I coded a step-by-step field trip sign-up mechanism for the education department, which facilitated thousands of schools reservations.I rewrote the frontend of the NHM calendar to make the experience more image based to give a users a visual description of the events. To improve accessibility I designed and coded a mobile frontend. I wrote a Drupal module to provide an API that powered various views of the calendar throughout the site. I worked with our digital designer to add a modernized basic template that uses a new menu that makes us of Drupal’s menu system so it could be maintained by Drupal’s GUI. I also designed and coded a new template for Research & Collections News.
